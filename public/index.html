OUR STORY

We would like to share a few of our cherished moments together, from the moment we first met up until when we got engaged. We will walk you through a couple of these moments in the timeline below.

15
may 2013

How we met

In all honesty we met a couple of months before this date, during a birthday party held by our common friend and maid of honour, Andreea. We did manage to talk for a bit, and we instantly got the feeling we each found a good friend. Since then, our common love for music brought us together to quite a few blues/jazz concerts. Mid May, we went to the seaside with a couple of friends and it was one of the best times of our lives. We all had a great time staying up late drinking, chatting, drinking, having fun and drinking.
30
jun 2014

Denisa leaves for Bern

Denisa works at the University and she is doing research in logics (“with unicorns” according to Vlad). After getting her PhD in Bucharest, she went to Bern for an 18 months postdoctoral stage. For her, this was an amazing adventure, both at the scientific and personal level. She gained a couple of good friends and a few great stories.
12
feb 2015

Vlad goes to Bern

We got back in touch in early 2015, and Denisa suggested a visit to Bern. Although flying isn't one of Vlad's pleasurable activities (he absolutely hates every second of it), he was looking forward to seeing Bern, and most of all, Denisa. He flew to Switzerland and shortly after, they became a couple. The next few months Vlad and Denisa flew back and forth between Switzerland and Romania quite a few times, until Denisa finished her postdoctoral stage in Bern.

23
nov 2015

And proposed

Denisa and Vlad went for a conference / vacation in Fiji in late 2015, which coincided with Denisa's birthday. Vlad had already prepared a present for her, which was - for all she knew, a perfume ( it's very hard to hide things from Denisa, so Vlad had to lie about the present). They had an absolutely wonderful time in Fiji, and on Denisa's birthday, Vlad proposed (and she said yes).
